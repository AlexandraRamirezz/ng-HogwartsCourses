How to get to HogwartsCourses?

To get to Hogwarts Castle, first go to King's Cross Station in London and look for the Nine and Three Quarters platform. Cross the barrier between the platforms to find her.

Then, board the Hogwarts Express, a special train that will take you to Hogsmeade station near Loch Ness.

Finally, take one of the small rowboats on the lake, which will take you to Hogwarts Castle.
